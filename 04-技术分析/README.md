# 04 | Technical Analysis

> Technical analysis studies "price action" — everything the market knows is reflected in the price.

---

## 4.1 Candlestick Charts — The Basics

Candlesticks are the language of technical analysis. You must master them.

```
          │  ← Upper shadow (High)
        ┌─┴─┐
        │   │  ← Body (Open to Close)
        │   │     Green/White = Close > Open (up day)
        └─┬─┘     Red/Black = Close < Open (down day)
          │  ← Lower shadow (Low)
```

### Key Candlestick Patterns

| Pattern | Meaning | Signal |
|---------|---------|--------|
| Hammer | Long lower shadow, small body | Bullish reversal at bottom |
| Shooting Star | Long upper shadow, small body | Bearish reversal at top |
| Doji | Open ≈ Close, upper & lower shadows | Indecision; potential trend change |
| Engulfing | Large body engulfs previous small body | Strong reversal signal |
| Three Soldiers / Three Crows | Three consecutive same-color candles | Strong trend continuation |

## 4.2 Moving Averages (MA)

Moving averages smooth out price fluctuations and show trend direction.

| MA | Period | Use |
|----|--------|-----|
| MA20 | 20-day | Short-term trend, intraday support |
| MA50 | 50-day | Medium-term trend, institutional focus |
| MA200 | 200-day | Long-term trend, bull/bear dividing line |

### Golden Cross vs. Death Cross
```
Golden Cross: MA50 crosses above MA200 → Bullish signal 📈
Death Cross:  MA50 crosses below MA200 → Bearish signal 📉
```

### EMA vs. SMA
- **SMA (Simple Moving Average):** Equal weight for all periods
- **EMA (Exponential Moving Average):** More weight on recent prices; reacts faster

## 4.3 RSI (Relative Strength Index)

RSI measures the speed and magnitude of price movements, ranging from 0–100.

```
RSI > 70 → Overbought zone (potential pullback)
RSI = 50 → Neutral
RSI < 30 → Oversold zone (potential bounce)
```

**Usage Tips:**
- Never use RSI in isolation — combine with price action
- In strong trends, RSI can stay above 70 for extended periods
- Look for **Divergence**: price makes new high but RSI doesn't = warning signal

## 4.4 MACD (Moving Average Convergence Divergence)

MACD = EMA12 − EMA26; reflects trend momentum.

```
MACD line crosses Signal line (upward)   → Buy signal
MACD line crosses Signal line (downward) → Sell signal
Histogram growing larger                 → Trend strengthening
Histogram shrinking                      → Trend weakening
```

**Deep Dive:**
- [Investopedia MACD](https://www.investopedia.com/terms/m/macd.asp)

## 4.5 Bollinger Bands

Bollinger Bands = MA20 ± 2 standard deviations

```
Price touches upper band → Potentially overbought, consider selling
Price touches lower band → Potentially oversold, consider buying
Bands squeeze (narrow)   → Low volatility; a big move is coming
Bands expand             → Volatility increasing; trend confirmed
```

## 4.6 Support and Resistance

This is the most fundamental concept in technical analysis:

```
Resistance: A price zone above that the market struggles to break through
Support:    A price zone below that the market struggles to break through

Rule: Once resistance is broken, it becomes support
      Once support is broken, it becomes resistance
```

**How to Find Support and Resistance:**
- Historical price levels touched multiple times
- Round numbers ($100, $150, $200)
- Moving averages (MA50, MA200)
- Prior highs and lows

## 4.7 Volume Analysis

**Golden Rules:**
```
Price up + Volume up   → Healthy trend, high confidence ✅
Price up + Volume down → Weak rally, watch for reversal ⚠️
Price down + Volume up → Heavy selling, trend may continue ⚠️
Price down + Volume down → Selling pressure easing, potential stabilization ✅
```
