## 7.1 Choosing a Broker

### U.S. Stock Brokers (for international investors)

| Broker | Min. to Open | Highlights | Best For |
|--------|-------------|-----------|---------|
| [Tiger Brokers](https://www.tigersecurities.com) | $0 | Chinese UI, HK/US stocks | Chinese-speaking users |
| [Futu / Moomoo](https://www.moomoo.com) | $0 | Feature-rich, great data | Intermediate users |
| [IBKR](https://www.interactivebrokers.com) | $0 | Lowest fees, institutional-grade | Larger portfolios |
| [Schwab/TD](https://www.schwab.com) | $0 | Largest U.S. broker, Thinkorswim | Options traders |
| [Tastytrade](https://tastytrade.com) | $0 | Purpose-built for options | Options-focused |
| [Robinhood](https://robinhood.com) | $0 | Simplest, commission-free | First-time investors |

### Core Selection Criteria
1. **Regulatory compliance:** Must be regulated by SEC/FINRA
2. **Account safety:** SIPC insurance (up to $500,000)
3. **Trading fees:** Stock trades mostly commission-free; check per-contract cost for options
4. **Platform features:** Charts, options chain, research tools
5. **Deposit/withdrawal:** International users must consider currency conversion
